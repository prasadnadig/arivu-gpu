## Thumbnail Infographic Concept (YouTube)

Create a **single thumbnail infographic** for a YouTube video titled: **Demystifying GPUs - Inside RTX 5090** .

Goals:
- Tone: easy-going, clear, non-intimidating.
- Audience: beginners interested in understanding TOPIC.
- Style: dark-mode thumbnail, bold title, very simple layout.

Include in the description:

- Layout:
  - 16:9 landscape composition.
  - Large central or top text: MAIN_THUMBNAIL_TITLE (e.g., “Demystifying GPUs” / “GPU Evaluation 101”).
  - One short subtitle/tagline: TAGLINE (max 4–5 words, e.g., “No Hype. Just Facts.”).
  - 1–2 simple icons or visuals:
    - e.g., CPU vs GPU icons.
    - or A100 vs H100 silhouettes (if relevant).
  - One minimal callout (e.g., “Stop trusting core counts alone”, “Beyond VRAM size”).

- Colors:
  - Dark background (charcoal / navy).
  - Accent colors (teal, cyan, orange) for emphasis.

Do NOT include any NotebookLM branding or logo in the slides or thumbnail.
