- A100: data-center workhorse, MIG, deployment/cost trade-offs  
- H200: modern heavyweight for LLMs, transformer engine, when to jump  
- Decision framework: prototype vs production, throughput vs latency

Ideal for AI engineers, data scientists, and tech enthusiasts making hardware decisions.
